When the Dials Overlap

Because your nervous system operates on a mixing board, the dials don't take turns. They can be turned all the way up at the exact same time. This creates blended states that are incredibly common for Autistic and ADHD nervous systems, but almost entirely absent from traditional models. Select a state to explore what is happening inside.
