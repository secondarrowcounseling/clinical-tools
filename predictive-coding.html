🧠 Neurotypical filter active. Strong priors suppress irrelevant data automatically.

The Neurotypical brain maintains a stable, narrow prior - a confident internal model of what to expect. Sensory data that matches predictions is compressed and ignored. Only genuine prediction errors reach conscious attention. This is why NT brains can work in a noisy cafe without hearing every conversation.
