How Your Brain Predicts the World

Your brain doesn't just sit back and experience the world in real-time. To save energy, it constantly runs 'Internal Scripts' based on past experiences, guessing what a room will sound, look, and feel like. It only alerts you when there is a 'Plot Twist', or a mismatch between your internal script and the raw data. This tool maps out how different neurotypes configure their script volume versus their raw data feed.
