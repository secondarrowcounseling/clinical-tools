The Mind-Body Intercom: How Your Brain and Gut Talk to Each Other

Your brain and your gut are constantly talking to each other through a two-way intercom system. It isn't just a one-way street; your emotions can change how your stomach feels, and your digestion can completely alter your mood. When life stress or physical sensitivities turn the intercom volume up too high, both systems start to panic. This tool shows how your internal settings create your unique mind-body mix.

🧠 Neurotypical Profile: Balanced gut-brain communication

Stable serotonin signaling maintains regular gut motility. Vagal communication is bidirectional and balanced. Interoceptive signals are accurately processed. The brain receives gut information as data, not danger.
