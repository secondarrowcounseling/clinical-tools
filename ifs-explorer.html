A Note

Every part of your system makes sense.

IFS doesn't ask you to eliminate any part, it asks what that part is carrying, and what it was trying to protect. The goal is understanding, not removal. When parts feel safe enough, they naturally step back and let the Self lead.

This tool is for psychoeducational purposes only and does not constitute clinical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It utilizes the Internal Family Systems (IFS) model developed by Dr. Richard Schwartz and the IFS Institute, and has been adapted by Second Arrow Counseling to ensure a neurodiversity-affirming approach.
