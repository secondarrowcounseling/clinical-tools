A Note

Every part of you makes sense.

In IFS, there are no bad parts — only parts doing their best with what they learned. Some strategies may not be working anymore, but they all started as protection. The goal is never to eliminate any part, but to understand them and lead from your core Self.

This tool is for psychoeducational use only and does not constitute clinical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Internal Family Systems (IFS) is a therapeutic model developed by Dr. Richard Schwartz / IFS Institute. Adapted for client introduction by Second Arrow Counseling.
